I am a mathematician building systems across robotics, perception, control, and machine learning. My preferred mode of work is to start from first principles, formalise assumptions, and then carry the model through to implementation and measurable behaviour.

I care about problems where estimation, decision-making, and control must operate together under real constraints. That includes visual localisation, feedback control for embodied systems, and learning pipelines that remain interpretable enough to debug and improve.

My academic background is in Mathematics at Imperial College London, with further postgraduate study in Finance. The finance and startup context informs how I think about technical bets and product pathways, but my core identity is as a builder of rigorous technical systems.
