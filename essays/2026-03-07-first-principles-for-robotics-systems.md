Robotics systems fail at interfaces: sensing to estimation, estimation to control, and model assumptions to real-world noise. First-principles work is less about elegance for its own sake and more about making each interface inspectable.

For me, that means writing down assumptions early, testing them against logs, and using experiments to revise the model rather than bypassing it.
